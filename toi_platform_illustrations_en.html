Illustration 3
User Journey Through the Platform
1
Registration and Data Entry
The couple registers on the platform. They enter the real monthly income of both families, the estimated number of guests, and the wedding date.
⏱ 5 minutes
2
Smart Calculator → "Safety Zone"
The algorithm calculates the maximum budget without debt burden. It shows: what you can afford and what you cannot. It offers a comparison: "standard toy" vs. "your safe alternative".
📊 Personalised Calculation
3
Mediation Module — Family Negotiations
Both families see the same calculation. The neutral platform helps them agree on a format before conflicts arise. "If both sides agree to a modest format — it frees everyone from debt" (P9).
🤝 Joint Decision
4
Marketplace: Choosing Verified Vendors
The platform suggests specific tamadas, photographers, restaurants, and decorators within the agreed budget. All vendors are verified and rated by the community.
🛍️ Matched to Your Budget
5
Outcome: A Dignified Toy Without Debt
Tradition preserved. The family took on no credit. A year later — the young family invests the money saved into housing or education, rather than paying off interest.
✅ Financial Stability
